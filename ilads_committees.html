Steering

Mark Christel

Mark Christel is the director of libraries at the College of Wooster. Christel also chairs the steering committee for a Mellon Grant devoted to the development of curricular digital projects in the Five Colleges of Ohio: Wooster, Denison, Kenyon, Oberlin, and Ohio Wesleyan.

Janet Simons

Janet Simons is the co-director of the Digital Humanities Initiative (DHi) at Hamilton College, where she has en involved in the planning and implementation of many of the digital projects produced through DHi. She has presented far and wide on topics that range from technical infrastures to instrcutional design.

William Pannapacker

As Director of Hope College's Mellon Scholars initiative, William Pannapacker has led the way with a model of student-led digital scholarship. Pannapacker is also the Director of the GReat Lakes Colleges Association (GLCA)'s Digital Liberal Arts Initiative.
